Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-29

This Privacy Policy explains how Flash Beacon (the “App”) handles information. The App is published by Bojan Drenovac (Austria), operating under the name Lighthouse Studios. If you have questions, contact: support@lighthousestudios.app.

Summary

  • The App does not require an account or sign-in.
  • We do not ask for your name, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.
  • Purchases (paid download and any in-app purchases) are processed by Apple.
  • If you email support, we will receive the information you send us.

Information We Collect

1) Information you provide

Support emails: If you contact us, we receive your email address and the message content you send, and any attachments you include. We use this only to respond and provide support.

2) Information collected automatically

The App is designed to work without collecting personal data. However, depending on your device settings and Apple’s platform features, certain technical information may be processed by Apple (for example, for App Store downloads, purchase validation, or fraud prevention).

3) Purchases

If you purchase the App or make in-app purchases, the transaction is handled by Apple. We do not receive your payment card details. We may receive basic purchase confirmation information necessary to unlock content or restore purchases (for example, whether a purchase is active).

How We Use Information

  • To provide the App’s functionality.
  • To respond to support requests and communicate with you if you contact us.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We share information only in the following cases:

  • Apple: for App Store distribution and in-app purchases.
  • Service providers (if used): If we enable third-party services (for example, backend services, crash reporting, analytics, or purchase infrastructure), those providers may process limited technical data to provide their services.
  • Legal requirements: if required to comply with law or valid legal process.

Data Retention

We keep support emails as long as necessary to handle your request and for reasonable record-keeping. If you want us to delete your support correspondence, contact us at support@lighthousestudios.app.

Your Rights

Depending on your location (including the EU/EEA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can exercise these rights by contacting us at support@lighthousestudios.app.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us via support email, contact us and we will delete it.

International Transfers

If you contact us by email or if third-party service providers are used, your information may be processed in countries outside your own. Where applicable, we rely on appropriate safeguards.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above will reflect changes.
